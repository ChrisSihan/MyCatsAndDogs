💌 I know that getting a pet for the first time can be a big challenge for a newbie, but the joy pets bring us always helps us overcome these difficulties. Below is a little introduction to pets of different age groups, hoping it can help you~

✨1st KITTEN

【One month of age】
1. Breastmilk is the mainstay for the first period or goat's milk formula is used instead.
2. Pay attention to the cold and warmth, cold weather can be homemade cat nest, prepare heating lamp or electric blanket, or empty bottle with some hot water on the side.
3. Try not to go out to avoid stress and infection.
4. Do not feed human food.
5. When the milk teeth come out, you can start to wean the cat slowly and gradually transition from liquid to semi-solid food.

【2 months old】
1. Transition from semi-solid food (soaked soft cat food) to hard food
2. Teach cats to go to the toilet and use cat litter.
3. Prepare to deworm the cat, full 6 weeks greater than 1 catty can do the first internal deworming, the general frequency of 3 months / times
4. Feed regularly and develop good eating habits.
5. Do not bathe the kitten as much as possible

【3 months old】
1. Staple food is cat food, goat milk powder can be used as nutritional supplements
2. At this time, the kitten's digestive system is still weak, in the process of switching to hard food, more observation of the poop situation
3. You can eat 1-2 egg yolks per week to help hair growth.
4. Prepare the cat bag/air box, and the kitten can go to the hospital for the first vaccination of the cat triple vaccine (mandatory) after 2 months.
5. Deworming and vaccination should be done one week apart, 1 month/times.

【4 months old】
1. During the rapid growth period of the body, choose a cat food with high protein and stable quality control to ensure adequate nutrition.
2. You can eat a small amount of boiled chicken breast for nutritional supplementation.
3. Be careful not to change the cat food frequently, and feed the cat regularly.
4. Prepare probiotics to prevent soft stools and diarrhea.
5. After 3 months, cat food does not need to be softened to avoid double rows of teeth, and eating hard food will help in the tooth replacement stage.

【5 months old】
1. Canned food can be prepared to adjust the flavor of the cat to supplement water
2. At this stage, the cat will start to change its teeth, and the milk teeth may be swallowed by the cat, so if you are lucky, you will be able to pick them up.
3. Cat's claws are itchy, so prepare cat scratching boards and cut nails regularly.
4. Put away fragile items at home to prevent your cat from breaking them. Broken glass is difficult to clean thoroughly, and it is easy to stick into the cat's flesh pad. Flesh pads of cats

【6 months old】
1. After the end of tooth replacement, you can try to brush your cat's teeth and develop the habit of brushing, which can effectively prevent oral diseases 2. The cat is changing its coat at this time, so you can start combing the cat's hair.
3. Cats are very naughty and curious at this stage, so you can interact with your cat more often and seal the windows to prevent accidents.

【7 months old】
1. This is the best time to train your cat
2. Freeze-dried food can be used for training and rewarding cats, as well as supplementing nutrition.
3. Don't give cats human food, and cats should not eat too much cold food.
4. Cats at this time sleep a lot and like to nap

【8 months old】
1. need to ensure that the cat's water intake, if the cat does not like to drink water you can buy a water dispenser to try
2. This period of time, the cat's diet is relatively stable, weight loss is a normal phenomenon
3. Curious about everything, need positive guidance
4. Pay attention to the daily cleaning of the ears to avoid breeding bacteria

【9 months old】
1. Regularly groom your cat to avoid licking into the stomach and causing hairballs.
2. Start to chemically treat your cat's hair, try to plant cat grass which is cost-effective, but if your cat doesn't like to eat it, you can buy cat grass tablets.
3. Try to eat less indigestible food and ensure daily water intake.
4. If aggressive behavior occurs, teach patiently

【10 months old】
1. Give cats abundant space and keep their moods happy
2. Pay attention to correcting the cat's bad behavior
3. Let the cat exercise more, and control its weight
4. Regularly comb the cat's hair and bathe it, and pay attention to blow drying it thoroughly after bathing to avoid catching a cold. Try to buy a slightly silent hair dryer, otherwise the sound is too loud and will easily scare the cat and stress it.

【11 months old】
1. During this period, the cat should have already started to come into heat
2. It is recommended not to let the female cat give birth too early, because the young female cat at this time is not physically and psychologically ready to have a baby, which is not good for health, and it is best to consider it after adulthood
3. It can exercise the cat's social skills
4. Canned food can also be arranged on a regular basis to increase water supplementation and avoid urinary diseases

【one year old】
1. has entered adulthood, life physical has stabilized
2. Do not scold the cat for no reason, it will be counterproductive to the cat can not understand, you need to guide the owner correctly
3. Use positive and correct training methods when training cats to cultivate their good habits.
4. After the cat is de-sexed, the cat's weight needs to be controlled to avoid overweight, which is detrimental to its health.
5. Annual medical checkups can be conducted


✨2nd PUPPY
【One month of age】
0-2 weeks the dog is like a baby, need to take care of the mother to clean up feces, drink breast milk to produce maternal antibodies. 2-4 weeks the dog began to open their eyes, began to slowly learn to walk, and around the curiosity of the dog.

【2 months old】
Immunization can be started, four, six, eight can be chosen, the first year of immunization needs to be three shots, each shot interval of 21 days!

【3 months old】
Entering the social learning stage, this is also the time to get familiar with the new environment to the new family, you need to pay attention to the puppy's physical and psychological changes due to the change of environment.

【4 months old】
Rabies vaccine can be injected at this time, and it is also an awkward period for hair change.

【5-6 months old】
The baby's teeth changing period is here, like people, only change teeth once in a lifetime, after the growth of 42 teeth Oh!

【7-8 months old】
It's time for dogs to reach the stage of sexual maturity, at this time, they will have a strong interest in the opposite sex and like to make friends. It is also a critical time for parents to choose whether to mate or neuter their fur babies. Oral care is on the agenda!

【9-10 months old】
Gradually integrate into our big family, learn good habits such as going out to defecate, make good friends and have high energy every day.

【11-12 months old】
Dogs are becoming adults and big kids, this is the time when their bodies and minds are maturing and they start to pay attention to medical checkups.

💌 Here are some other tips!


【Oral Care】
Oral care for pets can choose to brush teeth, teeth cleaning powder, mouthwash, teeth grinding snacks, etc. no matter which method must be adhered to, to ensure that your pet's dental health.

【Immunization Vaccine】
Different pets have different vaccination times, be sure to pay attention to the vaccination on time, if the vaccination period pets have come to your home, be sure to observe for two weeks to ensure that there is no problem with eating, drinking and defecating before immunization, to avoid the incubation period of viral diseases!

【Rutting period】
Sexually mature pets can be mated to produce offspring or sterilized to prevent future problems. Pets that are not sterilized will always be affected by the level of sex hormones, and girls stimulate the mammary glands as well as the uterus and ovaries, which can easily lead to reproductive system diseases. Boys are in heat and urinate all over the place and take over the territory to take mates, resulting in prostate enlargement and hyperplasia.
